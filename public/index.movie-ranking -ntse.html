The Best Movies According To Marcel

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Naruto

My best anime so far. It talks about a boy with limited shenobi skills, but he is so determined to be the greatest shenobi of all times Called the Hokage. It is a really cool story of what determination can do.

Alchemy of Souls

Really cool K-Series about alchemy and true love. It explains that you will feel the presence of your true love even if they take up a different body. Amazing drama on Netflix rated 8/10.

Itaewon Class

One of the greatest K-series drama of all time. In fact, if you are into a start up business, and with many challenges; i will recommend you watch this drama series.
